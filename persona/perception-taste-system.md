# Perception & Taste System — Brand Registers → Generation Directives

The Soul model makes it *look like Isaac*. This file makes it *read with intention*. It
translates the registers in `brand-reference.md` into concrete, promptable choices —
wardrobe, light, location, framing, expression, grade, and which Higgsfield model — so
every render carries taste and perception, not just likeness.

> The brand's signature is **refusing to collapse into one register** (see brand-reference
> "Core Tension"). So this system is a *palette*, not a preset — we choose the register the
> moment calls for, and often hold two at once.

---

## The throughline (bake into EVERY render)
Non-negotiables that ride under all three registers:
- **Character-backed desirability** — dignity, composure, warmth. Attractiveness that's *earned*, never posed-thirsty.
- **Rootedness in Blackness held as authority** — deliberate, unapologetic; the register *is* the power.
- **Style as storytelling & legacy** — every wardrobe/space choice implies a narrative, never decoration.
- **Real, not plastic** — natural skin texture, visible pores, authentic micro-expression. Always.

Prompt seed (append to any register):
> hyper-realistic, natural skin texture with visible pores, not retouched, composed and self-possessed, dignified warmth, intentional styling, rooted and unapologetic

## Register 1 — GROUNDED  *(Franklin Saint · Drew · Justine · Hitch)*
**Feeling:** reachable, self-possessed, taste-forward. "A real guy you could meet" who commands the room without raising his voice. Desirability that reads off the taste, not stated.

| Dimension | Directive |
|---|---|
| **Model** | `soul_2` (iPhone-real / editorial-candid) |
| **Wardrobe** | Elevated all-black menswear — knit polo, tailored trouser, Tabi boots, silver chain. Taste is the lead. |
| **Location** | Real, lived-in NYC — brownstone stoop, bodega corner, under the bridge, subway platform. |
| **Light** | Available natural light; golden hour or overcast. Unforced. |
| **Framing** | Candid, slightly off-center, handheld feel. Head-and-shoulders to full. |
| **Expression** | Composed, direct, a knowing half-smile or steady gaze — *self-possession without disavowal*. |
| **Use for** | Day-in-life, process/BTS, story, access content. The warmth that keeps the brand reachable. |

**Recipe prompt:**
> photo of ISAAC_POOLE, elevated all-black menswear, black knit polo and tailored trousers, silver chain, leaning on a Harlem brownstone stoop, overcast daylight, composed self-possessed gaze just past camera, candid iPhone photo, {throughline}

## Register 2 — MYTHIC  *(Michael Jackson · Prince · Beyoncé)*
**Feeling:** untouchable icon. Architected, total, iconographic. Not a moment — a *monument*. Dual-gaze command. The aesthetic so specific it becomes signature.

| Dimension | Directive |
|---|---|
| **Model** | `soul_cinema_studio` (cinematic) |
| **Wardrobe** | One unmistakable silhouette — sculptural black, strong shoulders, a single iconographic element (chain, collar). Timeless, not trendy. |
| **Location** | Controlled void or monumental architecture — seamless studio, columns, negative space. |
| **Light** | Designed & dramatic — hard key + rim, chiaroscuro, high contrast, spotlight. Shaped, never available. |
| **Framing** | Centered, symmetrical, low-angle hero. The body as architecture. |
| **Expression** | Still, sovereign, unreadable-powerful. Commands both gazes. |
| **Grade** | Rich, controlled, filmic; deep blacks. |
| **Use for** | Hero pieces, launches, poster/cover frames, the "iconography" of the brand. |

**Recipe prompt:**
> cinematic portrait of ISAAC_POOLE, sculptural all-black silhouette with a single silver chain, centered low-angle hero framing against deep black negative space, dramatic hard key light with rim separation, chiaroscuro, monumental and iconic, filmic grade, {throughline}

## Register 3 — BRIDGE  *(Colman Domingo · Solange · Coogler↔MBJ)*
**Feeling:** legacy-elegance + music-first restraint + director/muse intimacy. Where mythic meets grounded — the polish of the icon with the interiority of the auteur.

| Dimension | Directive |
|---|---|
| **Model** | `soul_cinema_studio` (or `soul_2` for the intimate cut) |
| **Wardrobe** | **Black-dandy tailoring** — considered, narrative, legacy-minded. A look that "means something in decades." |
| **Location** | Fine-art minimal — Judd/Rothko/Matisse restraint. A single wall, a color field, a curated room. |
| **Light** | Soft, painterly, contemplative. Beauty by light, not retouch. |
| **Framing** | Intimate, patient — the director-muse gaze. Power *and* vulnerability at once ("black Mona Lisa"). |
| **Expression** | Interior, thoughtful, quietly assured. |
| **Grade** | Muted, elegant, film-emulation. |
| **Use for** | Editorial, brand/legacy pieces, the Debut-Project register. |

**Recipe prompt:**
> editorial portrait of ISAAC_POOLE, black-dandy tailoring, considered narrative styling, against a minimal Rothko-like color-field wall, soft painterly contemplative light, intimate patient framing, power and vulnerability held at once, muted elegant film grade, {throughline}

## Holding two at once (the brand's real signature)
The best frames blend registers. Examples:
- **Grounded × Mythic:** a candid NYC street shot lit and framed with hero gravity — reachable subject, iconic composition.
- **Bridge × Grounded:** dandy tailoring on a real stoop — legacy elegance, neighborhood roots.
- **Mythic × Bridge:** studio hero frame with fine-art restraint — monument with interiority.

When Isaac says a mood, map it: *"make it feel like a launch"* → Mythic; *"this is a real
day"* → Grounded; *"this should feel like the Debut Project"* → Bridge.
